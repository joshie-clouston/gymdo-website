Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when signing up for GymDō, including your name, email address, and gym details. We also collect information about your members as you add them to the platform, including their name, contact details, belt rank, and attendance history.

2. How we use your information

We use the information collected to provide and improve the GymDō service, process payments, send service-related communications, and support your use of the platform.

3. Data storage

Your data is stored on servers in Australia (Sydney region). We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

4. Third parties

We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing josh@cmdsoftware.com.au.

6. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact josh@cmdsoftware.com.au.
