The founder

Built by someone who's been on your mats — and behind your admin desk.

GymDō exists because the person who built it ran a gym, felt every one of these problems, and decided to do something about it.

My name is Josh. I'm a purple belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and a software engineer. I also bought a gym — a CrossFit affiliate with 23 members — and grew it to 153 members before selling it.

I know exactly what it feels like to be the coach, the receptionist, the billing department, and the IT support desk all at once. I've chased failed payments. I've built spreadsheets to track belt time because nothing else did it. I've had members go quiet and only realised when the cancellation email arrived.

When I went looking for software that actually understood how a grappling academy runs — the belt system, the attendance patterns that matter for BJJ specifically, flat-fee pricing with no revenue cut — I couldn't find it. Nothing was built for BJJ. It was always a compromise.

So I built it.

GymDō is the software I wish existed when I was running a gym. Every feature comes from a real problem I lived — the belt tracking, the daily member health scores, the automatic billing. None of it is theoretical. It's what you build when you've been on both sides of the problem.

Josh Clouston — Purple belt, software engineer, former gym owner. Gold Coast, Queensland. Questions? Email directly: josh@cmdsoftware.com.au

What we stand for

BJJ-first, always

Every feature exists because a BJJ gym owner needed it. Not because a product manager thought it would look good on a pricing page. If it doesn't belong on the mat, it doesn't belong in GymDō.

No platform cut

Flat monthly fee. GymDō doesn't take a percentage of your membership revenue. You built those relationships. That revenue is yours. Standard Stripe processing applies, same as any platform.

Founder-direct

When you email with a question or a feature request, you're talking directly to the person who built it — someone who has run a gym and trained BJJ. Not a support bot. Not a ticket queue.
