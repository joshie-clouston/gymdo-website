Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

30 days free, no credit card required. Full access to every feature from day one. Your billing only starts after the trial ends.

Do you help with migration from my current software?

Yes — and it's free. Send us your member list, your export from Mindbody, Glofox, or wherever you're coming from, and we import everything. Members, belt records, billing setup. You don't re-enter a single name. A personal onboarding call with Josh gets your class timetable and membership plans configured before you go live.

What happens if my gym grows past my plan limit?

We notify you before you hit your limit and move you to the next tier automatically. No surprise charges, no service interruption. You'll always know exactly what you're paying.

Are there any percentage or transaction fees on top?

No platform cut. Standard Stripe payment processing fees apply (around 2.7%) — the same as any platform that accepts card payments. GymDō earns from your subscription only, not a percentage of your revenue. Pricing is in AUD. GST-compliant invoicing included.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month, no lock-in contracts. Cancel from within the platform with one click. We don't make it difficult because we don't want you to stay unless GymDō is genuinely the right fit.

What is the money back guarantee?

If you're not satisfied in the first 30 days after your trial ends, email us and we'll refund your first payment. No questions, no forms to fill, no 30-day notice period.

Do all plans include all features?

Yes. No feature tiers, no add-ons, no upsells. Belt tracking, retention scoring, member app, analytics — all included at every plan level. You only pay more as your academy grows.

I'm locked into a contract with my current provider. Can I still switch?

If you're mid-contract, reach out anyway. We're happy to talk about timing, start the trial to get familiar with GymDō, and do the migration the moment you're ready to go live. We'll be here.

How long does setup actually take?

Most academies are live within 24 hours. The onboarding call takes under an hour. Your members can book classes the same day you go live.

I'm not very technical. Will I be able to run this?

Yes. GymDō is built for coaches and gym owners, not IT departments. If something trips you up, you're emailing Josh directly — not submitting a support ticket.

What if GymDō is missing a feature I rely on?

Tell us on the onboarding call. Every feature in GymDō was built because a real gym owner needed it. If it fits our focus on BJJ academies, we'll build it. Feature requests from pilot gyms go directly into the roadmap.

Can I get a demo before signing up?

Absolutely. Email josh@cmdsoftware.com.au and we'll walk you through the platform live — screen share, your questions, no sales pitch. Just the product.
