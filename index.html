Common questions

Every question you're probably thinking right now

What happens to all my existing member data?

We migrate it for you. Send us your spreadsheet, your export from Mindbody or Glofox, or whatever you're using. We import your members, their belt records, and their billing details. You don't re-enter a single name.

How long does the switch actually take?

Most academies are live within 24 hours of starting. The migration call takes under an hour. Your members get a welcome email and can book their first class the same day.

My members are used to the current app. Will they cope with something new?

Members receive a welcome email with a direct download link and a one-tap login. The app is designed to be simpler than what they're probably using now. In our experience, members adapt faster than owners expect.

I'm not very technical. Will I be able to run this myself?

Yes. GymDō is designed for gym owners, not IT departments. If you can use Instagram, you can use GymDō. And if you get stuck, you're emailing Josh directly — not a ticket queue.

What if something breaks at 6am on a Tuesday when I have three classes running?

GymDō is hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure with 99.9% uptime. But if something goes wrong, you have a direct line to the founder in the same timezone. Not a US support team asleep at 11pm.

You're a new product. How do I know you'll still be here in two years?

Fair question. Josh is a software engineer who built and sold a gym — this isn't a side project. GymDō is his full-time focus. The pilot program with five QLD academies is how we're proving it before scaling. And if something ever changed, you can export all your data anytime.

What about my kids programs and parent sign-ins?

Parents can manage their child's membership and handle bookings through the member app. Family accounts link multiple children under one parent login. This is on the roadmap and being built based on pilot feedback.

What if GymDō doesn't have a feature my current software has?

Tell us on the onboarding call. Every feature in GymDō came from a real gym owner's real problem. If it matters to your academy and fits our ICP, we'll build it.
