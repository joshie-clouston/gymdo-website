How retention scoring works

1

Attendance patterns are monitored

Every member's attendance frequency is tracked week-by-week. Declining frequency is the primary signal.

2

Risk score is calculated daily

Each member gets a score from 0 to 100. Higher means higher risk of cancellation. Updated every day.

3

High-risk members are flagged

The retention dashboard surfaces your highest-risk members so you know exactly who to check in on this week.

4

You reach out at the right time

A personal message from their coach, sent before they cancel, is worth far more than a win-back campaign after they've left.
